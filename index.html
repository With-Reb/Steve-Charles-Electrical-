About Us

Steve Charles Electrical Ltd began with Steve working independently across the local area, building a reputation through straightforward, reliable work. What started with one person and a toolkit quickly became a trusted name known for clear communication, tidy results, and turning up when promised. As demand grew, so did the team. Skilled electricians and engineers joined over time, each bringing the same commitment to doing the job properly and respecting every home or workplace they enter. Today, the company handles everything from small domestic callouts to full commercial installations. We specialise in fire alarm systems, AOV (Automatic Opening Vent) installations, electric vehicle chargers, and all general electrical work — delivered with the same practical, honest approach the business was built on. Our focus remains simple: work safely, stay reliable, and leave every space better than we found it.
